NMIXX MEMBERS PROFILE

>

NMIXX (엔믹스) (formerly known as JYPn) is a 7-member South-Korean girl group under JYP Entertainment that consists of Haewon, Lily, Sullyoon, Jinni, BAE, Jiwoo and Kyujin. They debuted on February 22, 2022 with the single album “AD MARE“.
